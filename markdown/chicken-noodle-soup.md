---
title: chicken noodle soup
category: entree
subcategory: soup
ingredientGroups:
  - label: mirepoix
    items:
      - quantity: 1 or 2
        name: carrots
      - quantity: 1
        name: onion
        note: preferably yellow
      - quantity: 2 or 3 stalks
        name: celery
        note: or to taste
      - quantity: 3 or 4
        name: thyme stems
        note: or to taste
      - quantity: 4 tbsp
        name: olive oil
        note: or as needed
  - label: soup
    items:
      - quantity: 8 cups
        name: chicken stock
      - quantity: lots of 
        name: salt
        note: to taste
      - quantity: lots of 
        name: pepper
        note: to taste
      - quantity: 2 or 3
        name: chicken breasts 
      - quantity: 2 tbsp
        name: olive oil 
      - quantity: 2 to 3 cups pasta
        name: of choice 
      - quantity: 2 tbsp
        name: butter
        note: optional
      - quantity: 2 tbsp
        name: flour
        note: optional

steps:
  - Heat four tablespoons of [olive oil] in a pot on low heat.  
  - Slice the [carrots] and [celery] to desired thickness. Dice the [onion]. 
  - Place the [carrots], [celery], and [onion] in the pot and cook for about 5 minutes. 
  - Remove the leaves from the [thyme stems] and add to the pot. Cook another 5 minutes or until everything starts to soften. 
  - Add the [chicken stock], [salt], and [pepper] to the pot. Bring to a boil.
  - (Optional) Combine the [flour] and the [butter] until a smooth paste forms. Stir the paste into the pot. 
  - Add the [chicken breasts] to the pot. Pour two tablespoons of [olive oil] in a pan on medium heat. 
  - When the [chicken breasts] are almost cooked through, remove them from the pot and add them to the pan. Let them brown, flip, and let them brown again. Pull them apart with two forks and make sure they are cooked through. 
  - Add the [pasta] and pulled [chicken breasts] to the pot. Cook eight minutes or until [pasta] is al dente.
  - Serve warm.   
---
